Nutriflex logo assets (theme-aware)

Place your logo PNGs here so the app shows the correct version per theme:

- logo-light-full.png   = Nutriflex logo with BLUE "Nutriflex" text (used in light mode)
- logo-dark-full.png    = Nutriflex logo with PINK "Nutriflex" text (used in dark mode)
- logo-light-streamline.png = Icon/small version, blue (light mode)
- logo-dark-streamline.png  = Icon/small version, pink (dark mode)

The Logo component loads: /img/logo/logo-{mode}-{type}.png
(mode = light | dark, type = full | streamline)
